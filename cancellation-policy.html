Cancellation Policy:

We understand that plans can change unexpectedly. Below outlines our cancellation policy for bus ticket bookings:.
Cancellation Period: Customers can cancel their bus ticket booking up to 24 hours before the scheduled departure time.
Cancellation Fees:
- If canceled within 24 hours of booking and more than 24 hours before departure, a cancellation fee of 30% of the total booking amount will be charged. - If canceled less than 24 hours before departure, a cancellation fee of 80% of the total booking amount will be charged. - Any promotional discounts or coupons applied during booking will not be refunded.
Refunds:
- Refunds for canceled tickets will be issued to the original mode of payment within 2-8 business days after the cancellation request is processed.
No Shows:
- In the event of a no-show, where the passenger fails to board the bus without prior cancellation, no refund will be issued. - The ticket will be considered forfeited, and the passenger will not be eligible for any compensation or rescheduling.
Rescheduling:
-Customers may request to reschedule their booking to another available time slot or date, subject to availability. Rescheduling requests must be made at least 24 hours before the original departure time. -Any fare difference resulting from rescheduling will be charged to the customer's account.
Cancellation by the Operator:
- In the event of unforeseen circumstances or operational reasons, the bus operator reserves the right to cancel a scheduled trip. -In such cases, customers will be notified promptly, and a full refund will be issued to the original mode of payment.
Force Majeure:
- The company shall not be liable for any failure to perform its obligations under this policy if such failure is as a result of Acts of God (including fire, flood, earthquake, storm, hurricane or other natural disaster), war, invasion, act of foreign enemies, hostilities (regardless of whether war is declared), civil war, rebellion, revolution, insurrection, military or usurped power or confiscation, terrorist activities, nationalization, government sanction, blockage, embargo, labor dispute, strike, lockout or interruption or failure of electricity or telephone service.
By booking a ticket through our website, you agree to abide by the terms and conditions of our cancellation policy. We reserve the right to amend or update this policy at any time without prior notice.
